Telemedicine Consultations

Description

Our telemedicine service provides a virtual clinic experience. Patients can schedule appointments, discuss symptoms, receive diagnoses, and get electronic prescriptions without needing to visit a physical office.

Benefits

This service significantly reduces travel time and eliminates waiting room exposures to other illnesses. It is highly convenient for follow-up appointments, mental health counseling, and minor urgent care needs.

Tools & Technology Used

We utilize encrypted WebRTC protocols for secure video conferencing, integrated with our cloud-based Electronic Health Record (EHR) system to ensure patient data privacy complies with all healthcare regulations.
